About Me

I'm a 4th year Computer Game Science major at University of California, Irvine. I have been creating video games since 2018 and have developed 9 video games so far and 2 tabletop games. I am part of UCI's Video Game Development Club (VGDC) and have created several games within the club, some of which I was a team lead. I am currently working on an ambitious 10th video game in a team of 5 as part of my senior Capstone project with a mentor from Blizzard.

The game engines I use are Unity (C#) and Godot.
The programming languages I primarily use are C++, C#, Java, JavaScript, Python, and HTML/CSS.
Some additional software I am proficient in are Office 365, Git, and Adobe Creative Cloud (Illustrator, Lightroom, Photoshop, Premiere Pro), and Blender.
