About Me

Hi I'm Brooke, and I'm a video game developer. I love programming and UI/UX!

Video games have always been a huge part of my life and are a way for me to connect with friends and family and create fond memories. My love of video games inspired me to learn how to make them myself so that I can help others create meaningful experiences and connections through my own video games.

I have been creating video games since 2018 and have developed 12 video games so far and 2 tabletop games. I am part of UCI's Video Game Development Club (VGDC) and have created several games within the club, many of which I was a team lead. My favorite game that I've worked on is my ambitious senior Capstone project CraftAway, which was made in a team of 5 with the guidance of our mentor from Blizzard.

In my free time, I enjoy playing video games and board games, creating art, watching anime, and cuddling with my 2 adorable cats.
