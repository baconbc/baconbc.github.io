Hi I'm Brooke, and I'm a video game developer. I have experience in programming, UI/UX, and digital art.

About Me

I'm a 4rd year Computer Game Science major at University of California, Irvine. I have been creating video games since 2018 and have developed 8 video games so far and 2 tabletop games. I am part of UCI's Video Game Development Club (VGDC) and have created several games within the club, some of which I was a team lead. I am currently working on an ambitious 9th video game in a team of 4 as part of my senior Capstone project with a mentor.

The game engines I use are Unity (C#) and Godot.
The programming languages I primarily use are C++, C#, Java, JavaScript, Python, and HTML/CSS.
Some additional software I am proficient in are Office 365, Git, and Adobe Creative Cloud (Illustrator, Lightroom, Photoshop, Premiere Pro), and Blender.
